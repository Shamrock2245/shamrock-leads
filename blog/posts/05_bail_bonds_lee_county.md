## The Lee County Jail

The **Lee County Sheriff's Office** operates the main jail facility at **2501 Ortiz Avenue, Fort Myers, FL 33916**. This is where most arrests in Lee County are processed, including those from Fort Myers, Cape Coral, Lehigh Acres, Bonita Springs, Estero, and Sanibel.

### Booking Process

After arrest, the defendant is transported to the Lee County Jail for booking. This includes:

- Fingerprinting and mugshot
- Personal property inventory
- Medical screening
- Entry into the jail management system

Booking typically takes **3–6 hours**, depending on how busy the facility is.

### Inmate Search

You can search for an inmate on the **Lee County Sheriff's Office website** at [sheriffleefl.org](https://www.sheriffleefl.org). The online inmate search shows:

- Full name and booking number
- Charges and bond amounts
- Booking date and time
- Housing assignment

## How Bail Works in Lee County

### Bail Schedule

Lee County uses a **standard bail schedule** for most offenses. Common bail amounts include:

- **DUI (first offense)**: $500–$1,000
- **Simple battery**: $1,500–$2,500
- **Domestic battery**: $2,500–$5,000 (may include no-contact order)
- **Drug possession (felony)**: $5,000–$15,000
- **Burglary**: $10,000–$25,000
- **Aggravated assault**: $15,000–$50,000

For charges not on the schedule or when the defendant has prior history, bail is set at **First Appearance** — held daily at the Lee County Justice Center.

### First Appearance Schedule

First Appearance hearings in Lee County are held **every day at 8:30 AM**, including weekends and holidays. They're conducted via video link from the jail to the courthouse.

## Release Times in Lee County

After the bond is posted, release times at the Lee County Jail are typically **4–8 hours**. This varies based on:

- Time of day (overnight releases take longer)
- Current jail population
- Staff availability
- Whether there are other holds or warrants
