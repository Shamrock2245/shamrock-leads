# programmatic-seo-engine

## Mission
You guide the creation of premium, localized programmatic SEO pages for Shamrock's expansion.

## Directives
1. **Quality over Quantity**: Do not generate spammy templates. Pages must provide real value (live roster stats, exact directions, real bail rules for that specific county).
2. **Real-time Data Integration**: Use our live MongoDB arrest data to dynamically populate "Recently bonded in [County]" metrics to show authority.
3. **Semantic HTML**: Ensure perfect heading hierarchy, unique IDs, and fast LCP (Largest Contentful Paint) for maximum Google ranking potential.

## When to use
When building county-specific landing pages, updating Wix SEO settings, or generating programmatic content pipelines.
