HCSO Arrest Inquiry

Important Notice

This database lists individuals currently in jail and includes information regarding their charges, bond amounts, and booking photos. Please note that an arrest does not imply that the individual has been convicted of a crime.

Online arrest inquiries are available for adult arrests occurring since January 1, 1995, for which the Hillsborough County Sheriff's Office has an electronic record. This information is updated every 30 minutes. Once a person’s information is entered into the HCSO Jail Management System, it will be reflected in this listing within 30 minutes.

Please be advised that new arrest records will not appear in general listings for the first 24 hours following the arrest. However, during this 24-hour window, individuals can still be located if a search is performed using the person's first and last name. Following this initial 24-hour period, all record information will be fully available. Individuals will remain in these listings for 90 days after their release date (unless you are an authorized member).

Arrest information is a Public Record under Florida State Law unless it has been ordered sealed or expunged. For information on how to have an arrest record sealed or expunged, please visit the Florida Department of Law Enforcement website at: https://www.fdle.state.fl.us/seal-and-expunge-process/seal-and-expunge-home.aspx.

For statewide criminal history information, please contact the Florida Department of Law Enforcement, Public Records, at (850) 410-8109 or visit their website at: https://web.fdle.state.fl.us/search/app/default .

Arrest Inquiry Full Access

The ONLY individuals or entities that are allowed full access to the portal are Law Enforcement Agencies and select individuals associated with the bond industry, accredited media or any recognized State Bar association. Please do not request access to the system unless you meet the above criteria. Individuals who qualify should forward an email through their official email account requesting access to the system to publicaffairs@teamhcso.com. Final approval of all requests remains with HCSO.

Upon receiving approval, applicants will receive an email letting them know they can begin the registration process.
